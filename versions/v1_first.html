00.5 — Philosophy

01

Evidence before narrative

Systems shouldn't drift based on assumptions. Ground every layer — models, pipelines, knowledge bases — in verifiable inputs with explicit boundaries. If you can't point to the source, it's not real.

02

Ship, then iterate

A deployed agent that does one thing correctly beats a prototype that does everything in theory. I bias toward real deployments, real users, and observable outcomes from day one.

03

Hierarchies clarify everything

Good architecture is a taxonomy. Whether it's a Notion workspace, a cloud pipeline, or a community org — the right structure makes every operation obvious and every failure diagnosable.

04

Community is infrastructure

The best technical output comes from people who can coordinate. Running GDG Tulsa and NASA Space Apps isn't separate from the engineering work — it's how the ecosystem stays productive.
